May 18, 2022

Why 64?

It's the first question people ask me when they hear about 64 maths project. So why 64?


  1. 64 is the square of 8, the cube of 4, and the sixth power of 2. Quite an achievement already.
  2. It's the smallest number which is both a perfect cube and a perfect square. It brings it to the next level!
  3. Where all of us would have been without 64-bit computing?
  4. ...And Nintendo 64? Yes, I'm that old
  5. There are 64 squares on a chess board, the game I love and supposed to be good at. Some day...
  6. It's the subject of the Beatles' song: 'When I'm 64'. I love Beatles.
  7. 64 is the maximum number of strokes in any Chinese character.
  8. 64 is almost 69.
